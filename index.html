Irish Rail / Phone Gap demo app.

This is just a dummy/demo app for using phone gap. XML feeds are proxied through the yahoo YQL service (mainly just to get around the cross domain issues). Uses jQuery DataTables for display. Still a little slow and a few bugs, but proof of concept is there.
